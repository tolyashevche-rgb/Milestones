# Landing Structure: CDC x Europe Milestones

Version: Stage 4 draft
Date: 2026-05-05

## Goal

The landing page should convert a worried but thoughtful parent into either:

1. downloading the free `0-12 Months Starter Pack`;
2. opening the 6-month demo page;
3. joining the waitlist for the full 0-12 month pack.

The landing page must feel calm, evidence-informed and practical. It should not feel like a medical warning page, a course sales page, or a generic baby app.

## Primary Promise

> Your baby's first year, without the milestone panic.

Supporting copy:

> A calm, evidence-informed guide for parents in Europe: know what to watch, what to play, and when to ask.

## Above The Fold

### Left

- Product name: `Milestones Map`
- Headline: `Your baby's first year, without the milestone panic.`
- Subheadline: `Age-based observations, short play ideas and professional-friendly notes for 0-12 months.`
- Primary CTA: `Get the free starter pack`
- Secondary CTA: `Open 6-month demo`
- Trust line: `Aligned with CDC developmental monitoring, AAP surveillance principles, WHO nurturing care and Harvard serve-and-return mechanisms.`

### Right

Product preview, not a decorative hero:

- age selector: 2, 4, 6, 9, 12 months;
- `Watch / Play / Ask` tabs;
- progress checklist;
- one activity card;
- pediatric summary preview.

## Section 2: The Problem

Headline:

> The internet gives you fragments. Parents need a calm map.

Points:

- You see milestones in one place, activities in another and warnings somewhere else.
- A missed milestone should not create panic, but it should not be ignored either.
- Parents need a way to observe, support and prepare questions for professionals.

## Section 3: Method

Headline:

> Watch, Play, Ask.

Cards:

1. Watch: parent-friendly age observations.
2. Play: short activities with a `why it works` explanation.
3. Ask: professional-friendly notes when something concerns you.

## Section 4: What Makes It Different

| Existing Option | Helpful For | Gap |
|---|---|---|
| CDC tracker | trusted milestones | limited daily play and EU framing |
| Kinedu / BabySparks | daily activity plans | less transparent source-to-activity mapping |
| Parent forums | emotional reassurance | too much noise and anxiety |
| Our product | observation + play + evidence + professional summary | focused MVP, not diagnosis |

## Copy Guardrails

Use:

- calm map;
- parent-friendly observations;
- short play;
- professional-friendly notes;
- evidence-informed;
- concerns deserve attention.

Avoid:

- fix delays;
- prevent delays;
- guarantee outcomes;
- critical window;
- advanced baby;
- replaces pediatrician or specialist.
